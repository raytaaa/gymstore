Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

1. How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, click "Add to Cart," and proceed to checkout from the Cart page.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, and other major credit cards.

3. How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping usually takes 5-7 business days. Expedited options are available at checkout.

4. Can I cancel or change my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours by contacting customer support.

5. What if I receive a damaged product?

Please contact our support team immediately. We will arrange a replacement or refund for damaged items.
