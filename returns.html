Returns Policy

Our Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, we offer a 30-day return window from the date you received your item.

  • Items must be returned in original condition.
  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective.
  • Refunds are issued back to your original form of payment within 5-7 business days after receiving the return.
  • Clearance and final sale items are non-refundable.
  • To initiate a return, please contact our support team via the Contact page.
